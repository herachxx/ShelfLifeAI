The Problem

Why ShelfLife AI?

Food poisoning from expired products is a daily danger — especially for busy families, students, and working professionals. Most households have no clear picture of what's in their fridge: what's expired, what's fresh, and what they're missing. This costs money, health, and contributes massively to food waste.

ShelfLife AI turns any smartphone into a powerful fridge management system — complete with AI receipt scanning, expiry tracking, ingredient safety analysis, and family synchronization — addressing UN SDG 12 directly.

Our Ethical Commitments

  • Privacy First — No food data or photos are stored externally without explicit consent
  • Accessibility — Designed for all income levels; works fully offline without any paid API
  • Transparent AI — Users always know when suggestions come from AI vs. confirmed data
  • Zero Tracking — We never sell user consumption data to advertisers or third parties
  • Health Accuracy — All ingredient safety warnings are sourced from EU, WHO, and FDA databases
