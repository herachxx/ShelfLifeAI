What We Do

Everything your fridge
needs to stay smart

ShelfLife AI combines cutting-edge AI with a beautiful interface to make food management effortless for everyone.

📷

Receipt & Product Scanning

Take a photo of your grocery receipt or any product. Our AI instantly recognizes items, reads expiry dates, and adds everything to your virtual fridge.

⏱️

Expiry Countdown Timers

Every item gets a live countdown. Color-coded status: 🟢 Fresh, 🟡 Expiring soon, 🔴 Expired. Push notifications 48 hours before anything goes bad.

🤖

Smart AI Chat

Talk to your fridge naturally. Say "-1 egg", "finished the milk", or "what's expiring soon?" — the AI updates your list instantly.

🧬

Ingredient Analyzer

Scan any product label. Our AI highlights dangerous additives like E621, E250, palm oil, and allergens — protecting your family's health.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Fridge Sharing

Invite family members to share one fridge. Everyone sees live updates. Add notes like "My chocolate — don't touch! 😡" to protect your snacks.

🛒

Smart Shopping List

When items run out or expire, they automatically move to your shopping list. Prioritized by urgency so you never forget what matters most.

The Problem We're Solving

Why ShelfLife AI?

Food poisoning from expired products is a real, everyday problem — especially for busy students, families, and working professionals. Most people don't have a clear picture of what's inside their fridge: what's expired, what's fresh, and what they need to buy. This leads to both food waste and health risks.

ShelfLife AI addresses UN SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) by turning any smartphone into a powerful fridge management tool — complete with AI-powered receipt scanning, expiry tracking, ingredient analysis, and family sharing.

Ethics

Our Ethical Commitments

  • 🔒 Privacy First — No food data or photos are stored on external servers without consent
  • 🌍 Accessibility — Designed to work for all income levels, not just tech-savvy users
  • 🤖 Transparent AI — Users always know when AI is making suggestions vs. confirmed data
  • ♻️ Zero Tracking — We don't sell user consumption data to advertisers or third parties
  • 🏥 Safety Warnings — Ingredient alerts are sourced from verified health databases
