What We Offer

Everything your fridge
needs to stay smart

ShelfLife AI combines AI with a clean interface to make food management effortless for everyone.

📷

Receipt & Product Scanning

Take a photo of your grocery receipt or product. Our AI (powered by Gemini) instantly recognizes items and reads expiry dates — adding everything to your virtual fridge automatically.

⏱️

Expiry Countdown Timers

Every item gets a live countdown with color-coded status: 🟢 Fresh, 🟡 Expiring Soon, 🔴 Expired. Push notifications warn you 48 hours before anything goes bad.

🤖

Smart AI Chat

Talk to your fridge naturally. Say "-1 egg", "finished the milk", or "what's expiring soon?" — the AI parses your message and updates your inventory in real time.

🧬

Ingredient Analyzer

Scan any product label. Our AI highlights dangerous additives like E621, E250, palm oil, and allergens — protecting your family's health with every purchase.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Fridge Sharing

Invite household members to share one fridge. Everyone sees live updates. Add sticky notes like "My chocolate — don't touch! 😡" to protect your items.

🛒

Smart Shopping List

When items run out or expire, they automatically move to your shopping list. Prioritized by urgency so you always know what to buy first.

The Problem

Why ShelfLife AI?

Food poisoning from expired products is a real, everyday problem — especially for busy students, families, and professionals. Most people don't have a clear picture of what's inside their fridge: what's expired, what's fresh, and what they need to buy. This leads to both food waste and health risks.

ShelfLife AI addresses UN SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) by turning any smartphone into a powerful fridge management tool — complete with AI-powered scanning, expiry tracking, ingredient analysis, and family sharing.
